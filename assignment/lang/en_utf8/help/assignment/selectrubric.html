Rubric

You may use a rubric to coordinate the grading process of an assignment. The rubric will be visible to the student when viewing the assignment as well as available to your assistants for grading. When a rubric (located between the dashed lines) is selected, the grade dropdown is disabled. This is because the total grade is now based off the rubric's total points.

The rubric dropdown may be disabled to begin with. This will occur after an assignment has been created and a grade/feedback has been issued to the submissions of student's work.Therefore, please make any changes required before beginning grading (completing changes before the assignment's available submission date is best).
